## A/B test rules

- **Run cold ads for 7 days minimum** before judging. CPM stabilizes around day 4–5.
- **One variable at a time.** Don't change headline AND image AND CTA in the same test.
- **Kill below the floor.** If a variant's CPL is more than 1.5x the median, kill it after 100 clicks.
- **Lead-magnet CPL benchmark:** $2–$8 cold; $0.50–$2 warm retargeting.
- **Course conversion benchmark:** 1.5–3% of email list to Starter; 5–8% to Pro from your warmest 10%.
