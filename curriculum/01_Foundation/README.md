# Part 1 — Foundation

> The five modules that make every later workflow possible.
> Don't skip any. Don't rush them. The unlock for the entire course is sitting in here.

## Why this part exists

If your AI output sounds generic, the model is not failing you — three things are missing. This part installs them: the 4-folder system, the business context document, the data set, and the prompt structure that ties them together.

## Outcome

By the end of Part 1 you'll have:

- A working `AI_Mastery/` folder on your computer.
- A complete `business_context.md` (12 questions answered, no fluff).
- At least 50 of your past outputs sorted into `02_Data/` by type.
- A reusable prompt template you've already tested in Claude or ChatGPT.
- One concrete output (a tweet, a paragraph, anything) that sounds like *you*.

That's the foundation. Everything that follows leverages it.
