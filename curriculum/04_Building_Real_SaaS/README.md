# Part 4 — Building real SaaS

> Three production-shaped builds. The first one is full-stack with auth and real-time. The second has Stripe-gated usage. The third mixes vision-LLM with image generation.

## Why this part exists

Part 3 gave you the muscle. Part 4 puts it to work on projects that *look like real SaaS*: authentication, databases with row-level security, third-party APIs, async jobs, and Stripe-gated billing. By the end you'll have shipped at least one of these as something a real customer could pay for.

## Modules

| # | Lesson | Time |
|---|--------|------|
| [M15](M15_Project2_dashboard.md) | Project 2 — Client dashboard (full-stack) | 4–6 hrs |
| [M16](M16_Project3_lead_gen_saas.md) | Project 3 — Lead-gen SaaS | 6–10 hrs |
| [M17](M17_Project4_thumbnail_saas.md) | Project 4 — Thumbnail generator SaaS | 4–8 hrs |

**Total: 14–24 hours** of build time, depending on how thoroughly you customize each project. Most students do M15 (the muscle-builder) and *one* of M16 or M17 (the sellable build).

## Outcome

At least one full-stack SaaS project deployed with auth, persistent data, RLS-enforced security, and (in M16/M17) Stripe billing. A repo your future self could hand to a CTO.
