# Part 2 — Content workflows

> Five modules that turn the foundation into a publishing system.
> Tweets, long-form, short-form, and the engine that ties them together.

## Why this part exists

The foundation gives you the principle and the assets. Part 2 turns those into outputs. By the end, you'll have a sustainable cadence — not a one-off campaign that dies in week two.

## Modules

| # | Lesson | Time |

| [M10](M10_Content_rhythm.md) | Building your content rhythm | 30 min |

**Total: ~3.5 hours.**

## Outcome

By the end of Part 2 you'll have shipped:

- A 7-day batch of 7 tweets in your voice (M6).
- One ready-to-record long-form script with a House Style Guide (M7).
- 9 short-form scripts, 3 of them filmed (M8).
- A one-page Repurposing Plan converting one idea into six outputs (M9).
- A weekly content cadence on your calendar that you'll actually keep (M10).
